matplotlib.pyplot.uninstall_repl_displayhook

matplotlib.pyplot.uninstall_repl_displayhook()[source]

Uninstall the Matplotlib display hook.

Warning

Need IPython >= 2 for this to work. For IPython < 2 will raise a NotImplementedError

Warning

If you are using vanilla python and have installed another display hook, this will reset sys.displayhook to what ever function was there when Matplotlib installed its displayhook, possibly discarding your changes.
