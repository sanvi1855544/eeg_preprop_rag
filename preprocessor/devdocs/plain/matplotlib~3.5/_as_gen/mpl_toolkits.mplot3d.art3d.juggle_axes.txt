# mpl_toolkits.mplot3d.art3d.juggle_axes

mpl_toolkits.mplot3d.art3d.juggle_axes(xs, ys, zs, zdir)[source]

    
Reorder coordinates so that 2D xs, ys can be plotted in the plane orthogonal
to zdir. zdir is normally x, y or z. However, if zdir starts with a '-' it is
interpreted as a compensation for rotate_axes.
