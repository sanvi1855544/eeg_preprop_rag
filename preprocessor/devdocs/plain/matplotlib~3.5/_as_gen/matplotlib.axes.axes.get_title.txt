# matplotlib.axes.Axes.get_title

Axes.get_title(loc='center')[source]

    
Get an Axes title.

Get one of the three available Axes titles. The available titles are
positioned above the Axes in the center, flush with the left edge, and flush
with the right edge.

Parameters

    
loc{'center', 'left', 'right'}, str, default: 'center'

    
Which title to return.

Returns

    
str

    
The title text string.
