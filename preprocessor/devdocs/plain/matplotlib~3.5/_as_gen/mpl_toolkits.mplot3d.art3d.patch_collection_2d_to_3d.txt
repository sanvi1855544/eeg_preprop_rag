# mpl_toolkits.mplot3d.art3d.patch_collection_2d_to_3d

mpl_toolkits.mplot3d.art3d.patch_collection_2d_to_3d(col, zs=0, zdir='z',
depthshade=True)[source]

    
Convert a `PatchCollection` into a `Patch3DCollection` object (or a
`PathCollection` into a `Path3DCollection` object).

Parameters

    
za

    
The location or locations to place the patches in the collection along the
zdir axis. Default: 0.

zdir

    
The axis in which to place the patches. Default: "z".

depthshade

    
Whether to shade the patches to give a sense of depth. Default: True.
