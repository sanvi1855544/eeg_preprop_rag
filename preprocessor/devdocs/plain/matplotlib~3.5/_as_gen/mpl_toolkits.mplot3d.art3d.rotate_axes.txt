# mpl_toolkits.mplot3d.art3d.rotate_axes

mpl_toolkits.mplot3d.art3d.rotate_axes(xs, ys, zs, zdir)[source]

    
Reorder coordinates so that the axes are rotated with zdir along the original
z axis. Prepending the axis with a '-' does the inverse transform, so zdir can
be x, -x, y, -y, z or -z
