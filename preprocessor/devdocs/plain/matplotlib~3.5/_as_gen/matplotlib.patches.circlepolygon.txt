# matplotlib.patches.CirclePolygon

classmatplotlib.patches.CirclePolygon(xy, radius=5, resolution=20,
**kwargs)[source]

    
Bases: `matplotlib.patches.RegularPolygon`

A polygon-approximation of a circle patch.

Create a circle at xy = (x, y) with given radius.

This circle is approximated by a regular polygon with resolution sides. For a
smoother circle drawn with splines, see `Circle`.
