# numpy.ndarray.imag

attribute

ndarray.imag

    
The imaginary part of the array.

#### Examples

    
    >>> x = np.sqrt([1+0j, 0+1j])
    >>> x.imag
    array([ 0.        ,  0.70710678])
    >>> x.imag.dtype
    dtype('float64')
