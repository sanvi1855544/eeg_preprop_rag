# numpy.ma.MaskedArray.soften_mask

method

ma.MaskedArray.soften_mask()[source]

    
Force the mask to soft.

Whether the mask of a masked array is hard or soft is determined by its
`hardmask` property. `soften_mask` sets `hardmask` to `False`.

See also

`ma.MaskedArray.hardmask`
