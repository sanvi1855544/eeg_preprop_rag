# Module: tf.keras.applications.inception_resnet_v2

Inception-ResNet V2 model for Keras.

#### Reference:

  * Inception-v4, Inception-ResNet and the Impact of Residual Connections on Learning (AAAI 2017)

## Functions

`InceptionResNetV2(...)`: Instantiates the Inception-ResNet v2 architecture.

`decode_predictions(...)`: Decodes the prediction of an ImageNet model.

`preprocess_input(...)`: Preprocesses a tensor or Numpy array encoding a batch
of images.
