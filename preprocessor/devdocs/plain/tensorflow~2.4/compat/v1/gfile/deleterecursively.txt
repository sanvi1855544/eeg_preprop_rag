# tf.compat.v1.gfile.DeleteRecursively

Deletes everything under dirname recursively.

    
    tf.compat.v1.gfile.DeleteRecursively(
        dirname
    )
    
Args  
---  
`dirname` |  string, a path to a directory   
Raises  
---  
`errors.OpError` |  If the operation fails.
