# Module: tf.mlir.experimental

Public API for tf.mlir.experimental namespace.

## Functions

`convert_function(...)`: Import a ConcreteFunction and convert it to a textual
MLIR module.

`convert_graph_def(...)`: Import a GraphDef and convert it to a textual MLIR
module.
