# tf.experimental.numpy.asanyarray

TensorFlow variant of NumPy's `asanyarray`.

    
    tf.experimental.numpy.asanyarray(
        a, dtype=None
    )
    
Unsupported arguments: `order`.

See the NumPy documentation for `numpy.asanyarray`.
