# tf.experimental.numpy.trace

TensorFlow variant of NumPy's `trace`.

    
    tf.experimental.numpy.trace(
        a, offset=0, axis1=0, axis2=1, dtype=None
    )
    
Unsupported arguments: `out`.

See the NumPy documentation for `numpy.trace`.
