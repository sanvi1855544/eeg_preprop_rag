# tf.experimental.numpy.compress

TensorFlow variant of NumPy's `compress`.

    
    tf.experimental.numpy.compress(
        condition, a, axis=None
    )
    
Unsupported arguments: `out`.

See the NumPy documentation for `numpy.compress`.
