# tf.experimental.numpy.broadcast_arrays

TensorFlow variant of NumPy's `broadcast_arrays`.

    
    tf.experimental.numpy.broadcast_arrays(
        *args, **kwargs
    )
    
Unsupported arguments: `subok`.

See the NumPy documentation for `numpy.broadcast_arrays`.
