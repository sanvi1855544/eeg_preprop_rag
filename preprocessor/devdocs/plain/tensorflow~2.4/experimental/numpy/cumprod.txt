# tf.experimental.numpy.cumprod

TensorFlow variant of NumPy's `cumprod`.

    
    tf.experimental.numpy.cumprod(
        a, axis=None, dtype=None
    )
    
Unsupported arguments: `out`.

See the NumPy documentation for `numpy.cumprod`.
