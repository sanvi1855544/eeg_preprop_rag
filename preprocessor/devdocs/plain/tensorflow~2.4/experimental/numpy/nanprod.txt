# tf.experimental.numpy.nanprod

TensorFlow variant of NumPy's `nanprod`.

    
    tf.experimental.numpy.nanprod(
        a, axis=None, dtype=None, keepdims=False
    )
    
Unsupported arguments: `out`.

See the NumPy documentation for `numpy.nanprod`.
