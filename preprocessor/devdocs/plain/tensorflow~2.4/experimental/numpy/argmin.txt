# tf.experimental.numpy.argmin

TensorFlow variant of NumPy's `argmin`.

    
    tf.experimental.numpy.argmin(
        a, axis=None
    )
    
Unsupported arguments: `out`.

See the NumPy documentation for `numpy.argmin`.
