# tf.experimental.numpy.stack

TensorFlow variant of NumPy's `stack`.

    
    tf.experimental.numpy.stack(
        arrays, axis=0
    )
    
Unsupported arguments: `out`.

See the NumPy documentation for `numpy.stack`.
