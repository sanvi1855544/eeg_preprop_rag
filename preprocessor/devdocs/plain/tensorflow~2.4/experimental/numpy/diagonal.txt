# tf.experimental.numpy.diagonal

TensorFlow variant of NumPy's `diagonal`.

    
    tf.experimental.numpy.diagonal(
        a, offset=0, axis1=0, axis2=1
    )
    
See the NumPy documentation for `numpy.diagonal`.
