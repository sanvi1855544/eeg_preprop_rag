# tf.experimental.numpy.amax

TensorFlow variant of NumPy's `amax`.

    
    tf.experimental.numpy.amax(
        a, axis=None, keepdims=None
    )
    
Unsupported arguments: `out`, `initial`, `where`.

See the NumPy documentation for `numpy.amax`.
