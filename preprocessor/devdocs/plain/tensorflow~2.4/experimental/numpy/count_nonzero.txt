# tf.experimental.numpy.count_nonzero

TensorFlow variant of NumPy's `count_nonzero`.

    
    tf.experimental.numpy.count_nonzero(
        a, axis=None
    )
    
Unsupported arguments: `keepdims`.

See the NumPy documentation for `numpy.count_nonzero`.
