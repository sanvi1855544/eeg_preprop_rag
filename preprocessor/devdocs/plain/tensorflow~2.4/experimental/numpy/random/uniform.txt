# tf.experimental.numpy.random.uniform

TensorFlow variant of NumPy's `random.uniform`.

    
    tf.experimental.numpy.random.uniform(
        low=0.0, high=1.0, size=None
    )
    
See the NumPy documentation for `numpy.random.uniform`.
