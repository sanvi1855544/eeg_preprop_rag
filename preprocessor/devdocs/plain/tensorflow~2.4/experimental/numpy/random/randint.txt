# tf.experimental.numpy.random.randint

TensorFlow variant of NumPy's `random.randint`.

    
    tf.experimental.numpy.random.randint(
        low, high=None, size=None, dtype=onp.int
    )
    
See the NumPy documentation for `numpy.random.randint`.
