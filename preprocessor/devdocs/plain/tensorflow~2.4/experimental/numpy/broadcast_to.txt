# tf.experimental.numpy.broadcast_to

TensorFlow variant of NumPy's `broadcast_to`.

    
    tf.experimental.numpy.broadcast_to(
        array, shape
    )
    
Unsupported arguments: `subok`.

See the NumPy documentation for `numpy.broadcast_to`.
