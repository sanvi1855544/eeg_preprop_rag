# tf.experimental.numpy.isneginf

TensorFlow variant of NumPy's `isneginf`.

    
    tf.experimental.numpy.isneginf(
        x
    )
    
Unsupported arguments: `out`.

See the NumPy documentation for `numpy.isneginf`.
