# tf.experimental.numpy.full

TensorFlow variant of NumPy's `full`.

    
    tf.experimental.numpy.full(
        shape, fill_value, dtype=None
    )
    
Unsupported arguments: `order`.

See the NumPy documentation for `numpy.full`.
