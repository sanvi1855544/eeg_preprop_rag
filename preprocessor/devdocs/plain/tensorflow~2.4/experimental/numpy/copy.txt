# tf.experimental.numpy.copy

TensorFlow variant of NumPy's `copy`.

    
    tf.experimental.numpy.copy(
        a
    )
    
Unsupported arguments: `order`, `subok`.

See the NumPy documentation for `numpy.copy`.
