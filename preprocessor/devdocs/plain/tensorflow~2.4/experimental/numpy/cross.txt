# tf.experimental.numpy.cross

TensorFlow variant of NumPy's `cross`.

    
    tf.experimental.numpy.cross(
        a, b, axisa=-1, axisb=-1, axisc=-1, axis=None
    )
    
See the NumPy documentation for `numpy.cross`.
