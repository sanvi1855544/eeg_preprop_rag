# tf.experimental.numpy.isscalar

TensorFlow variant of NumPy's `isscalar`.

    
    tf.experimental.numpy.isscalar(
        num
    )
    
Unsupported arguments: `element`.

See the NumPy documentation for `numpy.isscalar`.
