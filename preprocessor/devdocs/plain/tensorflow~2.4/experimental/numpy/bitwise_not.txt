# tf.experimental.numpy.bitwise_not

TensorFlow variant of NumPy's `bitwise_not`.

    
    tf.experimental.numpy.bitwise_not(
        x
    )
    
Unsupported arguments: `out`, `where`, `casting`, `order`, `dtype`, `subok`,
`signature`, `extobj`.

See the NumPy documentation for `numpy.bitwise_not`.
