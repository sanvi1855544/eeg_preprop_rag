# tf.experimental.numpy.zeros_like

TensorFlow variant of NumPy's `zeros_like`.

    
    tf.experimental.numpy.zeros_like(
        a, dtype=None
    )
    
Unsupported arguments: `order`, `subok`, `shape`.

See the NumPy documentation for `numpy.zeros_like`.
