# tf.experimental.numpy.pad

TensorFlow variant of NumPy's `pad`.

    
    tf.experimental.numpy.pad(
        array, pad_width, mode, **kwargs
    )
    
Only supports modes 'constant', 'reflect' and 'symmetric' currently.

See the NumPy documentation for `numpy.pad`.
