# torch.Tensor.exponential_

`Tensor.exponential_(lambd=1, *, generator=None) → Tensor`

    
Fills `self` tensor with elements drawn from the exponential distribution:

f(x)=λe−λxf(x) = \lambda e^{-\lambda x}
