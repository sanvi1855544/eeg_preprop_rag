# torch.Tensor.to_sparse_coo

`Tensor.to_sparse_coo()` [source]

    
Convert a tensor to coordinate format.

Examples:

    
    >>> dense = torch.randn(5, 5)
    >>> sparse = dense.to_sparse_coo()
    >>> sparse._nnz()
    25
