# torch.Tensor.geometric_

`Tensor.geometric_(p, *, generator=None) → Tensor`

    
Fills `self` tensor with elements drawn from the geometric distribution:

f(X=k)=(1−p)k−1pf(X=k) = (1 - p)^{k - 1} p
