# torch.Tensor.sum_to_size

`Tensor.sum_to_size(*size) → Tensor`

    
Sum `this` tensor to `size`. `size` must be broadcastable to `this` tensor
size.

Parameters

    
size (int...) – a sequence of integers defining the shape of the output
tensor.
